Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide as accurate as possible prediction of what the weather will be like in the near future. On an everyday basis, many use weather forecasts to determine what to wear on a given day. Since outdoor activities are severely curtailed by heavy rain, snow and wind chill. Upfront Weather can be used to plan activities around these events, and to plan ahead and survive them..
