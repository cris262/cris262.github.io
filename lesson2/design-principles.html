White space can elevate the designs to a place of elegance and sophistication. Whether to highlight a message, convey simplicity or remove unnecessary distractions from the design.Shopify has used the white spaces pretty well to showcase it’s services in more profound way.

Hick's Law

Walmart

walmart.com

Walmart is an extremely popular company.Walmart provides many different choices to it’s customers, the more choices you have, the longer you take to make a choice and the more likely you’ll experience information overload. The more navigation options in a website can either be good or bad depending on the company. For Walmart it's been working because people tend to look for more things to buy and that quite forces them to navigate more on the site.

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

apple.com

Apple has a very good visual design, catching people's attention by just advertising it’sproducts with images. The human eye perceives information visually rather than as blocks of data. That is how designers influence users’ perceptions and guide them to desired actions.
